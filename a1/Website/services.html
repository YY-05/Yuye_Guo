Annual Dental Check-Up*

Physical Examination

We provide a thorough examination service to help you check your dental health. We help you check whether your teeth have any signs of illness, abnormalities, or discomfort.

Cleanings

Ensuring that your teeth are clean and free from plaque and tartar. You do not need to worry about yellow teeth or stains. We help you improve the beauty of your teeth.

Dental Check-UP

Dental Check-UP: We help you evaluate your dental health, including checking for signs of dental disease and we can also give you some good and suitable suggestions to achieve dental care.

Nutritional Guidance

We will discussion the daily diet with our patients and find out your nutritional needs. Then, we will give some advice to help you maintain a healthy smile.

X-Rays

Assessment and preventive measures against common dental issues.

Blood Tests or Laboratory Work

If you have dental history or specific health concerns, we may recommand additional diagnostic tests according to your age.

Other Services

We also provide dental implants, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, root canal treatment and emergency care.

We focus on patient education to ensure you have the knowledge and resources to make informed decisions about your dental health.
