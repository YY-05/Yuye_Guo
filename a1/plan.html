Goal

The website goal is to promote the business online, attract more customers in order to grow its revenue.

Success Evaluation

  • 1. Expanding online reach and client engagement:

    Metric: A minimum 10% increase in appointment requests or enquiries (via email, phone, or online form) within two months after launch.

    Evaluation Method: Track the number of new contacts and booking requests using Google Analytics event tracking and contact form submissions compared with the previous two-month average.

    Justification: Demonstrates that the website is successfully attracting and engaging a wider audience, including families, adults, and retirees.

  • 2. Promoting prebnetive dental care and annual check-ups:

    Metric: The “Annual Dental Check-up” information page should achieve a click-through rate of at least 15% of total site visitors and generate at least 20 booking inquiries per month through the promotion (20% off for first visit; 10% off annual check-ups).

    Evaluation Method: Use analytics tools to monitor page visits, click rates on promotional banners, and booking requests attributed to the campaign.

    Justification: Shows that the site effectively encourages patients to prioritise regular preventive care, aligning with the client’s long-term health mission.

  • 3. Building trust through staff and professional presentation:

    Metric: The “About Us” page (which highlights the clinic’s experienced staff) should maintain a click rate of at least 15% of total website visits and gather positive feedback mentioning professionalism or trust in at least 30% of online reviews.

    Evaluation Method: Use page analytics for visit tracking and sentiment analysis of user reviews or feedback forms.

    Justification: Indicates improved patient trust and interest in the professional qualifications of the dental team.

  • 4. Increasing awareness of dental knowledge:

    Metric: Educational article pages (e.g., “Importance of Regular Dental Check-ups”, “Oral Hygiene Tips”) should collectively receive at least 25% of total page visits and average over 1.5 minutes reading time per visitor.

    Evaluation Method: Use analytics tools to measure article page traffic and average session duration.

    Justification: Reflects success in fulfilling the client’s educational goal to inform patients and promote oral health awareness.

  • 5. Overall mission fulfilment:

    Metric: An overall user satisfaction rate of 80% or above, based on post-visit feedback surveys regarding ease of navigation, clarity of service information, and trust in the clinic.

    Evaluation Method: Conduct an online satisfaction survey linked to the “Contact” or “Thank You” page and review qualitative comments for improvement.

    Justification: Provides a holistic measure that the website achieves its mission of enhancing customer experience and supporting the clinic’s community-based dental care philosophy.

    • Target Audience

  • Family-oriented parents (ages 30–50):

    These are parents who seek a trusted local clinic that can provide routine dental check-ups, cleaning, and preventive care for both children and older family members. They prefer a website that is easy to navigate, clearly displays opening hours, contact details, and services, and offers convenient online appointment options.

  • Working professionals (ages 25–45):

    These clients are busy adults who may require restorative or cosmetic treatments such as fillings, orthodontics, or whitening. They expect a modern, mobile-friendly website with quick access to booking forms, service prices, and information about qualified dentists. The “Book Now” button and concise service summaries will help them make decisions quickly.

  • Older adults and retirees (ages 55+):

    This group values long-term care, comfort, and trust in the dental team. They will be attracted by sections showing professional experience, team credentials, and clear contact options. Larger font sizes and simple layout are also used to improve readability and accessibility.
